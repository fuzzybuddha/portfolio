I worked closely with the department supervisors to migrate the outdated website to a new responsive, mobile site using the university's WCMS. I organized the site into categories and subheadings, and hooked the site into the department's activenet database for a streamlined activity registration process for students. Before the department had this website, registration numbers were beginning to dwindle. This website gave students an easy way to view videos of past adventures and classes and significantly increased the number of registrations.
