3D Rendering

I used Blender open-source 3D rendering and animation software to create this short video of the earth.

I took the NASA maps for specularity, topography, atmosphere, bump maps and night light textures and mapped them onto concentric spheres to create a video of the earth turning in magnificent detail. You can see the world's night lights turn on as the terrain passes through shadowy portions of the image, and the atmosphere produces a gorgeous blue glow.

This was the final project for a class to demonstrate proficiency in 3D modelling, texture mapping, camera scrubbing, and animation. I modeled all of the assets in the scene from scratch and designed the textures in photoshop.
